Black Lives Foundation

THE FOUNDATION VISION

All children, particularly orphans, need a safe place to grow. Caring for orphans and nurturing them with the basics like food and clean clothes and access to education, increases the likelihood of them becoming positive members of their communities. Changing their narrative from being vulnerable to resilient.

The way in which these childhoods have been interrupted shall have long-term negative consequences on their emotional, cognitive, and social development. As such, our collective responsibility is to change our planet for a better future for our kids.

Our mission is a loving center for these poor kids who had lost their parents due to civil wars and global pandemics. This center is to provide generous support for these kids fostering, enormous capacity building for building, physical. Spiritual and emotional counseling healing for better growth for a better tomorrow.
